Let's horse around in support of Alzheimer's care and research.

Lingsch Realty is hosting a day at the races, in honor of Ed Lingsch and Alzheimer's & Brain Awareness Month. All proceeds from the event will be donated to the Alzheimer's Association. There will be a buffet lunch, live racing, a silent auction and much more! Come enjoy a timeless pastime, which Ed loved, while supporting an amazing organization.

About Ed

Ed was born in San Francisco on December 26, 1913, and passed away peacefully on November 12, 2011 at the age of 97. He was a graduate of Cogswell Polytechnical College, and longtime member of the Italian American Social Club of San Francisco. He founded Lingsch Realty in 1952, a San Francisco real estate company, where he worked until he retired at the age of 94. He resided for his last 49 years in Millbrae. Ed was a loving and generous father and grandfather, who always had a joke to tell and a smile on his face. He will be remembered by all for his sense of humor, strong work ethic and love of poker and the race track.

The Alzheimer's Association

The Alzheimer’s Association is the world’s leading voluntary health organization in Alzheimer’s care, support and research. The Association works on global, national and local levels to enhance care and support for all those affected by Alzheimer’s and other dementias. As the largest non-profit funder of Alzheimer's research, the Association is committed to accelerating progress of new treatments, preventions and ultimately, a cure.
